ON SITE VISIT , KANYAKUMARI

Kanyakumari, located at the southernmost tip of India, is famous for its beautiful beaches and scenic views. Recently, I had the opportunity to visit Kanyakumari to witness the implementation of an advanced boat monitoring system that utilizes artificial intelligence. It was an enlightening experience to see how technology is being used to enhance the safety of the fishermen and improve their livelihood. As I reached Kanyakumari beach, I was greeted by the beautiful blue waters and the sound of waves crashing on the shore. The boat monitoring system was set up by a team of scientists and engineers from a local research institute. The system was designed to monitor the movement of fishing boats in real-time using AI algorithms.

The system comprises a network of cameras and sensors that are installed on boats to monitor their movement, speed, and location. The data is then transmitted to a central computer system that uses AI algorithms to analyze the data and provide insights. The system can detect the presence of boats in restricted areas and send an alert to the authorities. This helps to prevent accidents and reduce the risk of fishermen getting lost at sea.
